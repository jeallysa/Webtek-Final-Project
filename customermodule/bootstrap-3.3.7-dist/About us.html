About Us

The tutorial website was originated from the idea that there exists a class of readers who respond better to online content and prefer to learn new skills anywhere by switch between computer, tablet, or mobile device. Simple Easy Learning is important, we encourage our readers acquire as many skills as they would like to. On this site, you will find a variety of tutorials that will help you to learn and use open source software. Academic tutorial gives appropriate academic advice and direction to help a student move on to subject they find hard. Languages tutorial will help people to learn foreign languages with free tutorials, grammar lessons, vocabulary lists, and audio practice. Instruments tutorial will help people to utilize the instruments to their full potential. Lastly, cooking/baking tutorial is available for people to be knowledgeable in some recipes/viand.



Mission

To deliver Simply Easy Learning with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of tutorials without any preconditions and impediments. Our aim is not to take you from "beginner" to "expert" but to take you from "beginner" to "comfortable".
